About Me

Megan Lacy is a current student at the University of Pittsburgh, with an expected graduation in April 2021. She is currently studying English Literature, with a specialization in global literature. She also is working towards a minor in Administration of Justice, and a certificate in Community and Corporate Relations. Megan has professional experience through the Honors Internship with the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), which she has been a part of since June 2019, and is currently still a part of the internship. Upon graduation, Megan hopes to pursue a career in either PR or Marketing. In her spare time, Megan enjoys running half marathons, dancing in the Pitt Dance Ensemble, and volunteering with Girls on the Run at a local elementary school.
